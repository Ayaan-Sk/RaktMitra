Blood Donor

In times of emergency, every second counts, and a timely blood donation can save lives. RaktMitra (रक्त मित्र) is a revolutionary platform dedicated to connecting blood donors with hospitals and patients in need. Our mission is to create a seamless, transparent, and efficient network where life-saving blood donations happen without delays. With real-time donor availability, emergency SOS alerts, and AI-powered demand prediction, we ensure that no life is lost due to a lack of blood. Whether you're a donor, a hospital, or someone in urgent need, RaktMitra is here to bridge the gap and make blood donation accessible to all. Join us in this noble cause and be a Mitra (friend) in someone's fight for life.
